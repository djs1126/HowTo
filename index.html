How to Make Meatloaf

In this assignment you will learn how to make a meatloaf in less than a hour.
Not only is it super easy and fast it, it is also delicious. Definetly a meal the whole
family will love!

What is needed:

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 can of vegtable soup
  • 1 cup of dry bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of water...NOT TABLESPOON

Steps:

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Dumb all the ingredients into a large mixing bowl.
  3. Mix all of the ingredients together with your hands.
  4. Once all of the ingredients are mixed, grease a bread pan
    and pack the meat mixture into the bread pan.
  5. Place wax paper over the bread pan.
  6. Microwave the meatloaf for 20-25 minutes on 50% power.
  7. Let the loaf cool and then serve and enjoy!

I hope you enjoy this easy meal. It sure has saved my family a time or two.
